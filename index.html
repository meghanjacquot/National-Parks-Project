National Parks: Places to Explore



National Parks
Are the Treasures of the People

There are over 400 National Park Sites in the United States of America. There are 62 locations known as National Parks.

These have a special designation.
National Park Sites are historic, natural, large, small, houses, battlefields, international, and so much more. They are protected by the men and women of the National Park Service and are for all people to enjoy.
